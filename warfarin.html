Warfarin

  • Continue Warfarin and adjust dose to achieve INR target of 2.0 (unless regional anaesthesia is considered where a target of 1.5 may be appropriate)

Indication for starting Warfarin

    • VTE within 3 months
    • Very high risk patients with a previous VTE whilst on therapeutic anticoag who now have a target INR of 3.5
    • Previous stroke/TIA in last 3 months
    • Previous stroke/TIA and three or more of the risk factors - CCF , HYPERTENSION, AGE>75, DIABETES
    • Patients other than those with Bileaflet aortic valve
    • And no other risks
    • Low Risk
    • Stop Warfarin 5 days before operation. Surgery can commence if INR is equal or less than 1.5 checked on the day of surgery
